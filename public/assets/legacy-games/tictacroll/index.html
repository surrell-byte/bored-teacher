How to Play 游戏说明
Learn every mode · 了解每种模式

Take turns placing your mark on empty tiles. First player to get the required number in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins the round.

轮流在空格上放置你的标记。第一个在横、竖或斜方向连成所需数量的玩家获胜。

How it works · 游戏方法
◈
Pick a tile选择格子Tap any empty tile to place your mark.点击任意空格放置你的标记。
🔄
Turns alternate轮流行动X always goes first. Players alternate every turn.X 先手，双方轮流进行。
🏆
Win the round赢得本局Get 3 in a row (3×3) or 4 in a row (larger grids) to win.3×3棋盘连成3个，较大棋盘连成4个即可获胜。
↺
Scores persist积分保留Win/draw counts carry over until you hit the new game button.胜负平的记录会保留，直到你点击新游戏按钮。

Each turn you roll the dice before placing your mark. The number you land on determines what happens — some rolls give you power-ups!

每轮行动前先掷骰子。骰子点数决定发生什么——某些点数会给你特殊能力！

Play solo against the computer. Choose a difficulty and your symbol, then try to outwit the AI.

单人挑战电脑。选择难度和你的符号，然后尝试智胜AI。

Difficulty levels · 难度等级
🌱
Easy简单AI moves almost randomly. Wins are rare but possible.AI几乎随机走棋，赢它不难。
⚡
Normal普通AI takes winning moves and blocks yours. A fair challenge.AI会抢占获胜位置并阻止你，是公平的对手。
💀
Hard困难On 3×3 the AI is unbeatable. On larger grids it plays strong heuristic strategy — the best fight you'll get.3×3棋盘上AI无法被击败；较大棋盘上AI采用强力策略，是最大挑战。
Tips · 技巧
💡
Corners first先占角落On 3×3, grabbing corners early limits the AI's options.在3×3棋盘上，尽早占据角落可以限制AI的选择。
🛡️
Watch for forks注意双重威胁On larger grids, set up two winning threats at once.在较大棋盘上，同时制造两个获胜威胁。

Choose your grid size from the main menu before starting a game. Larger grids need 4 in a row to win, adding depth and longer matches.

在主菜单中开始游戏前选择棋盘大小。较大的棋盘需要连成4个才能获胜，游戏更有深度，对局更长。

Available sizes · 可选大小
⬛
3×3 — Classic3×3 — 经典The original. Get 3 in a row to win. Fast rounds, pure tactics.经典玩法。连成3个获胜，节奏快，纯粹策略。
🟦
5×5 — Medium5×5 — 中等Get 4 in a row. More room to manoeuvre, longer games.连成4个获胜。空间更大，策略更多，对局更长。
🔷
6×6 — Large6×6 — 大型Get 4 in a row across 36 tiles. Strategy becomes essential.在36个格子中连成4个。策略变得至关重要。
🌐
7×7 — Epic7×7 — 史诗Get 4 in a row across 49 tiles. The ultimate test of patience and planning.在49个格子中连成4个。对耐心与规划的终极考验。
