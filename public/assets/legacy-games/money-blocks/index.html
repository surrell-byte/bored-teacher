How to Play

Two players take turns picking hidden tiles from the board. Each tile conceals a coloured block — and every colour triggers a different event. First to reach $1,000,000 wins. If the board empties first, the richer player takes the table.

💵
Green — Gain
Bank a cash windfall of $25K–$150K. A hidden multiplier may double or triple the amount.
💸
Red — Loss
Suffer a setback of $25K–$100K, with a chance the multiplier magnifies the hit.
🥷
Blue — Raid
Steal $25K–$100K directly from your opponent's account. Blocked if they hold a Guard.
💎
Yellow — Wild
Draw a wild reward — $250K, $500K, or double your entire holdings instantly.
🛡️
Purple — Guard
Raise a shield. Your next Raid from the opponent is automatically deflected.
🎲
Black — Wild Card
Spin the wheel: Jackpot (+$300K), Inheritance (+$500K), Swap (exchange balances), Encore (extra turn), Audit (-$200K), Robbery (-$150K), or Bankrupt (halved).
🔠 Each tile shows a letter A–Z. The letter is a mystery — it gives no clue about the colour hidden beneath.
⚡ A hidden multiplier (×2 or ×3) lurks behind some tiles — revealed only after you click.
🌈 One tile is Rainbow — a special Black wild card with animated colour.
